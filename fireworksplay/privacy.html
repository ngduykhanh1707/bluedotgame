We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is handled when you use the App. This policy explains our data practices in detail and applies to all users of the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information from you through the App. However, third-party service providers we use to operate and improve the App, as well as features like multiplayer and sharing, may collect certain data on our behalf. The types of information that may be collected include:

1.1 Information Collected Automatically by Third Parties

  • App Activity and Game Progress: Data about your interactions with the App, such as game progress (e.g., scores, firework shows, mini-game activities like Online Football or Quick Draw Cowboy Gun), session duration, and in-game actions across maps (e.g., Town, West, Samurai, Horror Mansion).
  • App Info and Performance: Crash logs and diagnostics data to help us identify and fix technical issues.
  • Device or Other IDs: Information about your device, such as device name, operating system (e.g., Android version), browser type and language (if applicable), IP address, and mobile device identifiers (e.g., device ID, advertising ID, MAC address, IMEI).
  • Analytics Data: We use third-party analytics tools, such as Unity 3D, to collect anonymized data about App usage. This includes metrics like the number of users, session lengths, and feature usage (e.g., mini-games, firework customization) to help us improve the App.
  • Multiplayer Data: For real-time multiplayer features (e.g., teaming up or competing with friends), we or our service providers may collect data such as player IDs, IP addresses, and game interactions to facilitate online play and ensure a smooth experience.

1.2 User-Generated Content

  • Firework Shows and Creations: When you create and save firework shows using sets, shells, cakes, racks, or other items (e.g., customizable shells like Brocade, Chrysanthemum, or props like Torch, Snow Man), this data may be stored on our servers or those of our service providers. If you choose to share your creations with other players, this content may be associated with a player ID or username (if applicable).

1.3 Information You Provide Directly

  • Support Communications: If you contact us directly (e.g., via email at contact@simplaystudio.com for support), we may collect the information you provide, such as your email address and any details included in your message.

1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

  • The App does not collect sensitive personal information such as your location, contacts, photos, or financial data.
  • We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information like your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Note: We do not provide a mechanism for you to request data deletion because the App does not collect personally identifiable information. Data collected by third parties (e.g., Unity) or shared with other players (e.g., firework shows) is managed according to their retention policies or your sharing preferences.
